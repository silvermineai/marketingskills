# Blog Structure Patterns

Templates and patterns for different blog post types. Use the one that best matches the keyword's search intent.

## Pattern 1: How-To Guide (Informational Intent)

Best for: "how to [do something]" keywords

```
# How to [Achieve Outcome] [Qualifier]

[BLUF: 2-3 sentence hook + why this matters now]

[Featured snippet: 40-60 word direct answer]

## Why [Topic] Matters for [Audience]
### [Context point 1]
### [Context point 2]
### [Common misconception to address]

## How to [Do the Thing]: Step-by-Step
### Step 1: [Action verb] [Specific task]
### Step 2: [Action verb] [Specific task]
### Step 3: [Action verb] [Specific task]

## [What to Watch Out For / Advanced Tips / Common Mistakes]
### [Pitfall or tip 1]
### [Pitfall or tip 2]
### [When to escalate or get help]

[Conclusion: restate BLUF, one clear next action]
```

## Pattern 2: Comparison / "Best Of" (Commercial Investigation Intent)

Best for: "best [tools/methods]" or "[X] vs [Y]" keywords

```
# Best [Category] for [Audience] in [Year]

[BLUF: 2-3 sentence hook + what we evaluated]

[Featured snippet: table or bulleted list of top picks]

## How We Evaluated [Category]
### [Criterion 1 — e.g., ease of use]
### [Criterion 2 — e.g., pricing model]
### [Criterion 3 — e.g., integration options]

## Top [N] [Category] Compared
### [Option 1]: Best for [Use Case]
### [Option 2]: Best for [Use Case]
### [Option 3]: Best for [Use Case]

## How to Choose the Right [Category]
### [Decision factor 1 — e.g., team size]
### [Decision factor 2 — e.g., budget]
### [Decision factor 3 — e.g., technical skill]

[Conclusion: restate recommendation, link to relevant service]
```

## Pattern 3: "What Is" / Explainer (Informational Intent)

Best for: "what is [concept]" keywords

```
# What Is [Concept]? [Benefit or Context]

[BLUF: 2-3 sentence hook + why reader should care]

[Featured snippet: concise definition in 40-60 words]

## [Concept] Explained
### [Core definition with examples]
### [How it works in practice]
### [Key components or types]

## Why [Concept] Matters for [Audience]
### [Benefit 1 with data]
### [Benefit 2 with real-world example]
### [What happens without it]

## How to [Get Started / Implement / Apply] [Concept]
### [First step]
### [Key decision point]
### [Resources or tools]

[Conclusion: restate definition, one clear next action]
```

## Featured Snippet Formats

The featured snippet paragraph is the most important paragraph in the post. Format it to match what Google is currently rewarding for the keyword:

### Paragraph Snippet (most common)
40-60 words that directly answer the question. Write as if an AI assistant would read it aloud.

### List Snippet
Bulleted or numbered list of 4-8 items. Works best for "steps" or "types of" queries.

### Table Snippet
2-4 columns, 3-6 rows. Works best for comparisons, specifications, or reference data.

## Heading Rules

- H1: One per post, in frontmatter `title` field
- H2: 3 main sections (topical pillars of the post)
- H3: ~3 per H2 (scannable sub-points)
- 3-4 total headings should be phrased as questions
- Include the primary keyword in at least one H2
- Include LSI keywords in H3s where natural
